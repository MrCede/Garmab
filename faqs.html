Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What services does Garmab Clean Services offer?

A

We offer residential, commercial, deep cleaning, post-construction, carpet cleaning, and specialty services based on your needs.

Q

How can I schedule a service?

A

You can schedule a service by calling us or sending us a message through our social media.

Q

What areas do your services cover?

A

Contact us to confirm if we are available in your area.

Q

Do you have flexible hours?

A

Yes, we offer flexible cleaning hours to suit your needs, including weekends and holidays.

Q

Is it necessary to be home during cleaning?

A

It is not necessary, but you can be present if you prefer. We only need access to the areas to be cleaned.

Q

How do you calculate the cost of the service?

A

Prices are based on the size of the space, the type of service required, and the frequency of service. We offer customized quotes.

Q

Do you offer recurring services?

A

Yes, you can schedule weekly, biweekly, or monthly cleanings with special discounts for recurring services.

Q

How can I pay?

A

We accept cash payments, bank transfers.

Q

What should I do before they arrive?

A

We recommend that you pick up personal items and make sure pets are in a safe place to make it easier for our team to work.

Q

Do you offer last-minute services?

A

Yes, depending on availability. Contact us to verify.

Q

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

A

Yes, as long as it is at least 24 hours in advance, otherwise there will be additional charges.
